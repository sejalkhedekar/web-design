About Central Perk

Tradition since 1994

If you have never heard about the TV show Friends and the café is where the characters used to meet.One of the famous achievements has seen a successful establishment of a real Central Perk at the heart of New York City. Located at 199 Lafayette St. at the corner of Broome Street, this iconic space features a coffee shop and a museum that is well documented with historical arrays of Friends Show’s props and souvenirs. It is a beautiful place to visit for tour enthusiasts, who would love to have a firsthand experience with some popular items that were characteristic of this heroic TV show. Central Perk is not real, it was made only for the show.
